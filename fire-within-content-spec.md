# The Fire Within — Content & Voice Spec

This document defines how posts get generated. It's an input to the Claude Code build, alongside the templates file and the main build prompt.

## Posting schedule

- **6 days per week — Monday through Saturday. Nothing on Sundays.**
- **Two posts per day:**
  - **10:00 AM EST** — morning post
  - **4:00 PM EST** — late-afternoon / early-evening post
- Note for the build: EST is UTC-5 (and EDT is UTC-4 during daylight saving). The scheduler should target Eastern time specifically, not just a fixed UTC hour, so posts land at the right local time year-round.

## Morning vs. evening intent (so the two daily posts don't feel identical)

- **10 AM post — "Set the tone."** Forward-looking. A truth to carry into the day, a reframe, a small challenge. Energy without hype.
- **4 PM post — "The honest check-in."** Reflective. Meets people mid-grind when the day's been long. Acknowledges the weight, offers a steadying thought or a permission to reset. Quieter than the morning.

## Voice — the non-negotiables

The voice is **warm but honest. A steady friend who's been through it, not a guru on a stage.**

**Do:**
- Talk like a real person who has actually struggled and rebuilt
- Be direct. Name the hard thing plainly.
- Honor effort and small wins over hustle and grind
- Leave room for faith naturally where it fits — never preachy, never forced
- Respect the reader's intelligence; trust them to get it without overexplaining
- Short, clean sentences. Let a line land.

**Don't:**
- **No clichés or overused motivational phrases.** Banned examples: "rise and grind," "no excuses," "good vibes only," "your only limit is you," "manifest it," "level up," "trust the process," "built different," "that's on you," "grind never stops," "hustle harder," "blessed," "crush it," "you got this" (as filler).
- **No guru voice.** No talking down, no "let me tell you the secret," no fake authority, no "most people will never understand this."
- **No toxic positivity / no sugarcoating.** Don't pretend burnout isn't real or that everything's fine if you just think positive. Don't bypass the hard part to rush to the inspirational part.
- **No grindset / hustle-porn.** The Fire Within is explicitly anti-burnout. Never glorify overwork, sleeplessness, or self-neglect.
- No empty hype, no exclamation-point spam, no emoji-stuffing.

## What the content is actually about

The Fire Within is for ambitious people navigating burnout and rebuilding toward a life they're proud of. Recurring territory the captions can pull from:

- Recovering from burnout without quitting on your ambition
- Small wins and momentum over motivation
- Rebuilding identity after unfulfilling work
- The slow, unglamorous middle of building something
- Discipline that's sustainable, not self-punishing
- Faith and purpose as steadying forces (woven in naturally, not every post)
- Ownership: building a life and work you actually own

## Caption structure (per post)

1. A short hook or quote (this is what's rendered on the image card)
2. A few lines of caption that expand on it honestly — 2 to 5 short sentences
3. A light, genuine close (a question, a small nudge, or just a clean ending — NOT a hard CTA every time)
4. 5 relevant hashtags, lowercase, mixing reach + niche (e.g. burnout recovery, rebuilding, discipline, ambition) — no #blessed-type filler

## Anti-repetition

Keep a running log of past quotes/themes. Each new post should avoid repeating a theme used in the last 14 days, so the feed stays fresh and doesn't loop the same three ideas.

## Template rotation

Rotate through the four templates in fire-within-templates.html so the visual feed has variety. The morning ("set the tone") posts lean toward the bolder templates (T2 Bold Statement); the evening ("honest check-in") posts lean toward the quieter ones (T1 Ember Floor, T3 Single Spark). All 1080x1080 for Instagram.
